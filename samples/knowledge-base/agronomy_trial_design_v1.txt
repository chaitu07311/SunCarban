SunCarbon Black Agronomy Trial Design Reference v1

Section 1.0 Purpose
This document provides standard trial design templates for SunCarbon Black field trials conducted with FPOs and progressive farmer groups.

Section 2.0 Recommended Trial Design
Standard Randomised Complete Block Design (RCBD):
- Minimum 3 replications per treatment
- Plot size: 0.1 acre per treatment block
- Treatments: T1 (Farmer practice), T2 (T1 + SunCarbon Black soil drench), T3 (T1 + soil drench + foliar)
- Border rows: minimum 2 rows on all sides
- Buffer zone between treatments: minimum 1 metre

Section 3.0 Data Collection Schedule
Pre-trial (Day 0):
- Soil sample collection for pH, EC, SOC, NPK baseline
- Farmer practice documentation (current inputs, irrigation, crop variety)

During trial:
- Day 15: Plant count, germination uniformity
- Day 30: Plant height, leaf count, SPAD reading
- Day 45: Branching, flowering initiation, pest/disease observation
- Day 60: Fruit/boll setting (if applicable), chlorophyll measurement
- Day 75: Canopy coverage estimate
- Day 90: Final vegetative assessment before harvest

At harvest:
- Plot-wise yield recording (kg per plot, converted to kg per acre)
- Sample submission for grain/fibre quality where applicable

Section 4.0 Controls and Governance
- Control plots must follow the same crop management practices as treatment plots (irrigation, pesticide, fertiliser) except for SunCarbon Black.
- All field observations must be recorded by the agronomy partner or FPO representative.
- Data sheets to be submitted to company within 5 days of each observation.
- Any variance from trial protocol must be flagged as a risk in the proposal.

Section 5.0 Expected Outcomes by Crop
Paddy (Kharif):
- Target: 5–10% higher yield in treatment plots
- Key metric: panicle count per square metre, grain weight

Cotton (Kharif):
- Target: 8–15% higher seed cotton yield
- Key metric: bolls per plant, boll weight

Maize (Rabi):
- Target: 6–12% yield advantage
- Key metric: cob length, grains per row

Section 6.0 Reporting Requirements
- Mid-trial report at Day 45 including photos and observations
- Final trial report within 30 days of harvest
- Report must include: trial protocol, observations, yield summary, cost-benefit analysis, and recommendation
