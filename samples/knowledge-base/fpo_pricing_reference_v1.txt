SunCarbon Black FPO Pricing Reference v1

Section 1.0 Pricing Model
This document provides reference pricing guidelines for SunCarbon Black commercial proposals to FPOs and farmer groups.

Section 2.0 Base Pricing
Standard product pricing (ex-warehouse):
- Pouch (500g): INR 350 per unit
- Bag (5 kg): INR 2,800 per unit
- Bulk sack (25 kg): INR 12,500 per unit

FPO volume discount tiers:
- 50–100 kg order: 5% discount on ex-warehouse rate
- 101–500 kg order: 10% discount
- 501 kg and above: 15% discount (subject to approval)

Section 3.0 Trial Pricing (Pilot Programs)
For structured crop trials, the following indicative pricing applies:
- Trial input cost target: INR 400–600 per acre (including product + application)
- FPO pilot pricing: INR 3,200–4,000 per 5 kg unit (trial rate)
- Custom pricing for orders above INR 5 lakh requires national sales head approval.

Section 4.0 Payment Terms
- Net 30 from delivery for established FPOs with prior order history
- Advance 50% for new FPO partners on first order
- Credit notes issued within 7 days for shortage or quality complaints

Section 5.0 Cost-Benefit Reference for Proposals
Expected economic return per acre (cotton, Kharif, Maharashtra):
- Incremental yield: 60–90 kg seed cotton per acre (treated vs. control)
- Value of incremental yield: INR 3,000–4,500 at INR 5,500 per quintal
- Input cost of SunCarbon Black per acre: INR 400–500
- Net expected benefit per acre: INR 2,500–4,000

Note: These are reference estimates based on field trial data. Actual results depend on soil type, variety, season, and management practices. All commercial proposals must state this clearly.

Section 6.0 Assumptions to Validate in Proposals
- Confirm current market price for the crop in target geography
- Confirm input cost benchmark with FPO finance lead
- Validate trial acreage and logistics cost before pricing sign-off
